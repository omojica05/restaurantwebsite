About Us

We are a vegan cafe located in Lower Manhattan. We focus on American classics, recreating and making them more accessible to those with a plant-based diet. We are focused on providing clean, wholesome meals to the community, and are also allergen-friendly. By Chloe was founded by Chef Chloe Coscarelli back in 2014, before she was pushed out. After all these years, Chef Chloe is back in business, providing the same service and freshly baked veggie treats.
